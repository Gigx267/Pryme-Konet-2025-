Cultural Movement

The City Boy Campaign

A youth-driven initiative promoting oneness and national consciousness through the lens of Gwarinpa, Abuja. This campaign aims to unite young Nigerians by showcasing the vibrant culture, challenges, and aspirations of urban life in one of Africa's most dynamic cities.

Filmed in Gwarinpa Estate, the largest residential hub in West Africa, our message is simple: "Fight for your rights, one voice." This project dives deep into the heart of Abuja's bustling community, highlighting the stories of everyday heroes who navigate the complexities of modern Nigerian society.

We took our cameras to the streets—connecting with local traders, shoemakers, and youths to capture the raw energy of the Nigerian dream. Through candid interviews, street performances, and behind-the-scenes glimpses, we explore themes of resilience, unity, and the pursuit of a better future. Join us in celebrating the spirit of the city boy, where every voice matters in shaping the nation's narrative.
